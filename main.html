About Us

Your go-to spot for delicious pizza made with only the freshest ingredients. We're a family-owned restaurant with over 20 years of experience in serving the community. Our pizzas are made with love and care, using a wood-fired oven to bake them to perfection. We believe that the best pizza starts with quality ingredients, which is why we use only the freshest produce and highest quality meats and cheeses.

At 43 AVENUE, we offer a variety of classic and unique pizzas to choose from, including our famous pepperoni, margherita, and Hawaiian pizzas. We also offer a range of sides, salads, and desserts to round out your meal. We take pride in providing our customers with the best possible dining experience, whether you choose to dine in, carry out, or have your pizza delivered.

So come visit us at Pizza House, and taste the difference that quality ingredients and skilled craftsmanship can make in a pizza.
